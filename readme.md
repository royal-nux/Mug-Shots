# ☕ Mug Shots - BAC Campus Coffee

"A mug worth shooting for"

## About
Mug Shots is a coffee ordering service for BAC students, lecturers, and staff. Order your favorite coffee and get it delivered anywhere on campus!

## Features
- 🎓 Student discount (5%)
- 📍 Campus-wide delivery
- ⭐ Rating system
- 🚚 Collect or delivery options
- 👥 Role-based pricing (students, lecturers, staff)

## How to Use
1. Select your role (first-time visitors)
2. Add coffee items to your order
3. Choose delivery or collection
4. Provide delivery location (if delivery selected)
5. Rate your experience after receiving coffee
